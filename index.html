What is ScoobyDex Finance?

ScoobyDex Finance - $SOBY is an autonomous yield and liquidity generation protocol which is based on Binance Smart chain which will reward the community through the tokens in the ecosystem.

10%

ScoobyDex charges 10% tax on each transaction.

3%

tax is redistributed to all $SOBY holders.

7%

tax goes to liquidity forever after splitting it up 50/50 in BNB and $SOBY.

Features

Frictionless Staking.

3% of all transactions are redistributed to the holders. This is also known as “Auto-Staking”, by which the holders will earn tokens even without doing the actual Staking.

Lower Volatility with Auto-Locking

7% of each transaction is locked to liquidity forever. This contributes to lower volatility and a continuously increasing price floor.3.5% goes to LIQUIDITY as $SOBY+3.5% goes to LIQUIDITY as BNB

SCOOBY DEX.

$SOBY is the main asset that will be used with ScoobyDex Finance. Our project will be initially focusing to build our own Decentralized Exchange (DEX) based on Binance Smart Chain (BEP20), and additional features will be added along the way.
